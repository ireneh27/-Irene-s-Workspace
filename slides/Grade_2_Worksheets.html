LANGUAGE PATTERNS

Pattern 1: Comparison

_____ is healthier than _____.

Example: An apple is healthier than candy.

Pattern 2: Sugar Content

_____ has less sugar than _____.

Example: Yogurt has less sugar than ice cream.

Pattern 3: Giving Reasons

_____ is better because _____.

Example: Nuts are better because they have protein.
