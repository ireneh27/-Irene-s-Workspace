About Me

Hello! I'm Irene, an English teacher based in China, passionate about helping students of all levels master the English language.

My teaching philosophy centers on creating engaging, interactive lessons that make learning English both effective and enjoyable. I work with diverse groups of students, from beginners taking their first steps in English to advanced learners refining their skills.

I believe in using a variety of teaching methods and materials to cater to different learning styles. This website serves as a repository for my teaching materials, lesson slides, and resources that I've developed over the years.
